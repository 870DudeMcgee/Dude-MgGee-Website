## Verification Checklist

- Confirm the sender's identity through an official company or outlet domain.
- Visit the organization's website independently rather than only through message links.
- Verify the sender appears on the organization's staff, roster, or professional profiles.
- Ask for the exact deliverables, dates, costs, rights, term, and cancellation terms in writing.
- Treat urgency, guaranteed results, gift cards, crypto, wire-only payment, and secrecy as warning signs.
- Do not send identification, tax forms, banking information, stems, or unreleased masters until the party and agreement are verified.
- Have an entertainment attorney review any contract involving ownership, exclusivity, publishing, masters, management, or a long term.
